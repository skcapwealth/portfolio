About Us

At SKCAPWEALTH, our primary goal is to assist families in achieving their financial aspirations while ensuring their investments are well protected. We offer tailor-made financial solutions that align with each client’s risk appetite and long-term financial objectives. By understanding the unique needs of our clients, we design strategies that help safeguard wealth and support the achievement of important life goals. SKCAPWEALTH is an AMFI-Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-187439) and a Registered SIF Distributor with AMFI. We are also an APMI Registered PMS Distributor (APRN05531) and an IRDAI Licensed Insurance Agent, enabling us to provide a wide range of investment and protection solutions. We strongly believe in the power of long-term investing. Starting early and staying invested helps investors benefit from compounding returns and rupee cost averaging, allowing wealth to grow steadily over time and improving the financial security of individuals and their families. Our commitment is to guide clients in making informed financial decisions so that their hard-earned money works efficiently for them—today and in the future.

About Me

With over 12 plus years of experience in the financial services industry, I have had the opportunity to work with top financial institutions like Kotak Securities Limited, HDFC Bank, and Sundaram Mutual Fund. Throughout my career, I have helped retail and high-net-worth clients manage their investments and achieve their financial goals. I hold an MBA in Financial Services from Goa University and am a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) from FPSB- INDIA. After spending over a decade in corporate finance, I founded SK CAPWEALTH in 2021 with a vision to offer a client-first approach to financial planning, with a focus on providing holistic wealth management and protection solutions.
